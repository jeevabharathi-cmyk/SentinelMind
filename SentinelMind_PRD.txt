
SentinelMind – Insider Threat Prediction Platform

Product Overview:
SentinelMind is a commercial, privacy-preserving insider threat prediction platform designed to detect risky behavioral patterns before security incidents occur.

Objectives:
- Early detection of insider risk
- Privacy-first analytics
- SME and mid-enterprise friendly

Target Users:
- SOC Analysts
- Security Administrators
- Compliance Teams

Core Features:
- Secure authentication (RBAC + MFA)
- Log ingestion (API, CSV)
- Behavioral baselining (role-based)
- Risk scoring (0–100)
- Alerting and dashboards
- Tamper-proof audit logs

Non-Functional Requirements:
- <200ms API latency
- 99.9% availability
- Horizontal scalability
- GDPR-style compliance
